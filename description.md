# WaitMate: The Load Screen You'll Actually Want to See

**Tired of staring at a blank white screen while the internet takes its sweet time?** 

WaitMate is a premium Chrome extension that transforms those boring loading moments into a delightful, interactive "AI Thinking" experience. Instead of a flash of white, you're greeted with a sleek, minimalist dark overlay featuring witty, adaptive messages that reflect your browsing habits.

### ✨ Why you'll love WaitMate:

*   **Intelligent Personalities**: Our "AI" starts out funny, gets a bit impatient, and eventually goes through an existential crisis if the page takes too long.
*   **Site-Specific Wit**: Custom messages for your favorite sites. YouTube buffering? *"Buffering dopamine..."* Twitter loading? *"Loading opinions..."*
*   **Interactive Visuals**: A beautiful, mouse-reactive particle system and dynamic radial gradients make the wait feel premium.
*   **Speed Recognition**: If a page loads in under a second, WaitMate notices! Experience special "Fast Load" messages that celebrate your elite internet speeds.
*   **Zero Latency**: Built to be ultra-lightweight and injected at `document_start` to catch the screen before the first pixel of the page even loads.

### 🚀 Key Features:
- **Dark Mode Background**: Easy on the eyes, especially for late-night browsing.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: Modern design with subtle glows and micro-animations.
- **Mouse Attraction**: Particles that dance around your cursor while you wait.
- **Fake Progress Bar**: A sleek progress indicator that keeps the vibes high.
